Formerly known as UXLagos, usable is a community of people (designers, developers, enthusiasts) who are interested in designing usable products.

At our monthly meetups, we talk about UX and things related to it: interaction design, visual design, user research, sketching and prototyping. We also explore the role of UX in the development process, marketing, business, everyday life. We share our experience, we present our actual projects, we analyze trends and more.

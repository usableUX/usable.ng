A community of humans who are interested in building beautiful & functional products.

At our monthly meetups, we talk about UX and things related to it: interaction design, visual design, user research, sketching and prototyping. We also explore the role of UX in the development process, marketing, business, everyday life.

Since 2014, there has been over 40 usable meetups and the community has grown 600+ members spanning two cities (Lagos & Abuja).
